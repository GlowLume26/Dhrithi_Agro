🌾 About Drithi Agro

We're on a mission to transform Indian agriculture by connecting farmers directly with quality inputs, expert knowledge, and fair markets.

Our Story

Drithi Agro was founded in 2022 with a simple but powerful vision — to make quality agricultural inputs accessible to every farmer in India, regardless of their location or farm size.

We saw farmers struggling with counterfeit products, inflated prices, and lack of expert guidance. We decided to build a platform that solves all three problems at once.

  • Founded by a team of agri-tech enthusiasts and farmers
  • Headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra with pan-India operations
  • Backed by leading agri-tech investors
  • Serving 5 lakh+ farmers across 28 states
