❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I track my order?

Go to My Account → My Orders → Click "Track Order" on any order to see real-time tracking updates.

What is the return policy?

We offer a 7-day easy return policy on all products. If you receive a damaged or wrong product, contact us immediately.

How do I become a vendor?

Click "Sell With Us" in the header, fill the vendor registration form, upload required documents, and our team will review within 2–3 business days.

Are all products genuine?

Yes! All products on Drithi Agro are sourced directly from authorized manufacturers and verified vendors. We have a strict quality check process.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD). All payments are secured by Razorpay.

Is there free delivery?

Yes! We offer free delivery on all orders above ₹499. For orders below ₹499, a nominal delivery charge of ₹49 applies.
